# Runway — Zama Demo Script (10–15 minutes)

This is one specific, tested playthrough path chosen to hit all 4 real Missions, all 3
guaranteed emotional payoffs, the strongest available ending, and both NFT mint entry points,
inside a 10–15 minute live demo. Every step below was verified reachable in this exact
sequence during RC1 playtesting.

**Before you go on stage:** connect a funded Sepolia wallet ahead of time, and pre-run the
four underlying CipherOps actions (Registry wrap, Operations payment, Airdrop funding,
Vesting setup) a few minutes early so you have four real transaction hashes ready to paste —
this keeps live block-confirmation wait time from eating your 10–15 minute window. The
transactions are still real and still verified live in front of judges; only the timing of
executing them moves earlier.

---

## 0:00–0:30 — Cold open

Land on `/runway`. Say: *"This is Runway — a business simulation where finishing the story
means actually executing this exact product, live, on Sepolia. Nothing here is simulated."*

**Notice:** no menu, no page list — just the office and whoever needs you right now.

## 0:30–1:00 — Wallet

If not already connected, click **Connect Wallet** (top right) now, before entering any
scene. Confirm the connection in front of judges — it's fast and sets up everything after.

## 1:00–2:30 — Registry Mission (Kai)

Click **"See what they need."** Read Kai's line aloud or let it sit a beat — the problem is
stated before any feature is named. Click **Open CipherOps →**, paste the pre-run Registry
transaction hash into the verify field, click **Verify**. Show the real receipt (tx hash,
block, wallet). Choose **"Take the time, get it right."**

**Verbally explain:** *"Kai never says 'use Registry.' He states an impossible-sounding
constraint — has to exist today, can't be visible yet — and the tool is just the only
honest answer to it."*

When prompted, click **Mint Achievement & Continue** — this is the first live NFT mint judges
see. Confirm in wallet.

**WOW moment candidate:** the mint confirmation itself, if it's the first FHE-adjacent NFT
mint judges have seen tied to a real gameplay decision rather than a claim button.

## 2:30–3:00 — Contractor dilemma (Mara/Priya)

Back in the office, this scene should be next. Choose **"Pay her generously — and keep it
between the two of you."**

## 3:00–4:30 — Operations Mission (Priya)

**Open CipherOps →**, paste the pre-run Operations tx hash, **Verify**. Choose **"Send a short
personal note along with it."** Skip minting here — click **Continue** — and say: *"I'll show
the mint flow again in a minute from a different entry point."*

## 4:30–5:00 — Back Kai (Dana/Kai dilemma)

When Dana's one-pager scene appears, choose **"Back Kai — keep it need-to-know."**

**Verbally explain:** *"This is the branch that pays off at the very end — watch for it."*

## 5:00–5:30 — Mentor Theo

When Theo asks Kai to teach him, choose **"Tell Kai to actually take the time and teach him
properly."** This sets up both the strongest ending and the Kai/Theo emotional arc.

## 5:30–6:00 — The reporter call (Mara)

Choose **"Let it stand — figure out how to make it true."** This is what makes the Airdrop
Mission necessary next.

## 6:00–7:30 — Airdrop Mission (Mara)

**Open CipherOps →**, paste the pre-run Airdrop tx hash, **Verify**. Choose **"Make it
generous — cover everyone who asked, and then some."**

**Notice for judges:** Mara's line right before the CTA — *"we have to actually pay them, we
just can't let the amounts turn into a leaderboard"* — is the whole pitch for confidential
distribution, delivered with zero jargon.

## 7:30–8:00 — WOW moment #1: Mara's announcement

This scene appears automatically next. Let Mara's line land: *"I got to actually tell people
something's happening... without it turning into everyone doing math on each other's
numbers."* Choose **"Tell her that's the whole point."**

**What judges should notice:** she never says "encrypted." She just describes what it let her
do.

## 8:00–9:30 — Vesting Mission (Dana/Theo)

Read the scene text in full — Theo's line, *"Can it just be mine?"*, is one of the strongest
moments in the episode. **Open CipherOps →**, paste the pre-run Vesting tx hash, **Verify**.
Choose **"Set it up generously — he's earned it."**

## 9:30–10:00 — WOW moment #2: Theo's reveal

Appears automatically. Choose **"Ask him what it feels like."** Let his answer land: *"Like
the first thing that was ever just... mine, without an asterisk."*

**This is the single strongest emotional beat in the demo. Do not rush this scene.**

## 10:00–10:20 — WOW moment #3: Kai's realization

Appears automatically, ties directly back to the Dana/Kai branch from 4:30. Choose **"Tell him
that's the whole point."**

**Verbally explain:** *"Kai never mentions Dana by name here — but this is the system replacing
the need to trust a person's promise. That's the actual value proposition, dramatized instead
of explained."*

## 10:20–11:30 — The ending

The finale should now trigger (Kai's variant — the strongest available ending, unlocked by
backing Kai + mentoring Theo + completing the contractor arc). Let his line land in full.
Choose **"Same time next quarter."** for the warmer, more human close.

## 11:30–13:00 — Career Profile & second mint

Click **"Review your career"** from the episode-end screen. Show the achievement list — this
path should show roughly **9 of 14 unlocked (~64%), "Senior Operator."** Point out the
Mission/Milestone tags. Pick an unminted milestone achievement (e.g. **Perfect Mission** or
**Mission Completion**) and click **Mint this achievement** to show the second, independent
mint entry point. Confirm in wallet.

## 13:00–14:00 — Close

**Verbally explain, recap:** *"Every Mission in this game only exists because the story
couldn't be honestly resolved without it. The technology was never explained — Theo
discovered it by decrypting his own number. That's the difference between a demo of FHE and
a story that needed it."* Mention briefly that Episode 1 is Quarter 1 of a planned
multi-quarter arc, and that the achievement collection is designed to carry forward into it.

## 14:00–15:00 — Buffer / Q&A

Use remaining time for questions. If short on time earlier, the safest cuts are: the second
Career Profile mint (13:00 block) and the contractor/mentorship flavor beats (2:30–5:30) —
never cut a Mission verification or a WOW moment.
